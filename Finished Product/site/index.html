This is a website built from the ground up with one purpose in mind: ensuring that the people of Guilford County have access to information that will help them find the asistance they need. By partnering with many local non-profit organizations, we were able to deliver a product that is more efficient and less intrusive than previous methods. By completing the following registration form once, those in need will be able to get the asistance they need without having to fill out a form while waiting in line. Using this new website a family will only need to register once, and each visit after that they need only log into their account. This allows more people to move through the line, save the volunteers time, and the organizations money.
